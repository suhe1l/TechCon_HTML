History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts in Silicon Valley. What started as a 100-person meetup has grown into one of the most anticipated annual tech events, now hosting over 10,000 attendees from around the globe.

Over the years, we've expanded our focus from web development to encompass emerging technologies like AI, blockchain, and quantum computing, always staying at the forefront of innovation.

Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and knowledge-sharing in the tech community. We aim to:

  • Showcase cutting-edge technologies and their real-world applications
  • Provide a platform for networking and idea exchange among industry leaders and newcomers
  • Inspire the next generation of tech innovators through educational workshops and mentorship programs

Past Speakers

Maria Ahmed

Maria Ahmed, a pioneer in artificial intelligence, spoke at TechCon 2022 about the ethical implications of AI in healthcare. Her groundbreaking work has influenced AI policies worldwide.

Abu Taymiyah

Abu Taymiyah, a leading figure in cybersecurity, delivered a keynote at TechCon 2023 on the future of data protection in the age of quantum computing. His insights have shaped industry standards for secure communication.
